Spatio-Temporal Distribution of Burned Areas

Politecnico di Milano · CNR-IREA · Geoinformatics Engineering

Rishikesh Miriyala · Sriram Krishnasamy Seenivasan · Ghulam Abbas Zafari

Abstract

This study compares burned area estimates from global datasets (FireCCI54 and MCD64) using Google Earth Engine. Multi-year analysis reveals spatial and temporal patterns, evaluates the influence of climate and land use, and integrates various environmental indicators to enhance fire risk assessment and dataset reliability.
